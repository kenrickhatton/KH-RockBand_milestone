About Us

Want to know more about RockBand ? Here You can find some interesting information.

Our band was formed in 1965 in London, England. The founder is our world-adored drummer, Steve. I remember, once he said " I wanted to make some money and buy new car, not play around the world... "(laughing) - Eddy. Our crew changed many times but we still staying strong. Now our crew have 3 players and vocalist. We're playing in all europe, usa and sometimes in Japan and South Korea. We love to make crazy things on concerts to makes our public happy.
